About Dropwit

Our Mission

Dropwit is transforming the way goods move around cities by enabling anyone to have anything delivered on-demand. Our revolutionary platform connects customers with local couriers who can deliver anything from any stores in minutes. We empower communities to shop local with no waiting and empower businesses through our API to offer delivery.

Our Team

Dropwit is built by a passionate team of engineers, designers, operations and growth specialists, and is headquartered in San Francisco. The company was co-founded by Eric Williams, Sarah Woods, and Bill Lehmann in 2017.
